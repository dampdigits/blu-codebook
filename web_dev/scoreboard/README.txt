Scoreboard Webpage:

This webpage allows you to keep track of scores between two teams or two players. The scoreboard can be updated in real-time, with the ability to increase or decrease scores.

Features:

1. Record scores for two teams or two players
2. Increase or decrease scores in real-time
3. Reset scores to start a new game

Usage:

1. Enter the names of the two teams or players.
2. Use the “+” and “-” buttons to increase or decrease the scores.
3. Click on the “Reset” button to start a new game.

I hope you enjoy using this scoreboard webpage! Let me know if you have any feedback or suggestions for improvement.
